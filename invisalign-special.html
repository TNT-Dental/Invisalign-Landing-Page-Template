How Invisalign Works

Starting with a digital model of your mouth, we are able to utilize the latest technology to create a series of clear aligners. Used in sequence, these thin, plastic devices move your teeth into their ideal positions – quickly and painlessly. By starting with a computer model of your “perfect smile”, we can achieve amazing results predictably. Invisalign is an ideal solution for teens and adults, and everyone benefits from fewer trips to the dentist. Most importantly, fewer trips means time and money savings for everyone involved.
